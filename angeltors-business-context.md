## Company Overview

Angeltors (angeltors.com) is an angel-investing / startup platform operating in India (entries concentrated in Uttar Pradesh, especially Prayagraj/Allahabad). The platform runs a content blog (funding news, success stories, startup profitability updates) alongside forms for:
- **Invest With Us** — prospective angel investors registering to invest
- **Raise Capital** — startups applying for funding
- **Mentorship** — professionals offering to mentor startups
- **In-House Support** — professionals offering advisory/support services
- **Consultation / Contact / Leads / Newsletter Subscribe** — general inbound inquiries
